ParsedClassics project aims at creating integrated set of functionality allowing for reading classics with ease and pleasure:

  • best scholarship from public domain sources available at few mouse clicks;
  • morphological parsing, multiple lexicons, concordances, translations, commentaries, grammars, audio recordings;
  • scholarly aides in Greek, Latin, Italian, French, English and German;
  • possiblility of reading Greek texts using aides in Latin and vice versa;
  • possibility of reading classical texts using scholarly aides in descendant languages: in modern Greek for ancient Greek texts and in Italian and French for Latin texts;
  • most flexible interface allowing to arrange texts and scholarly aides according to personal preferences.
