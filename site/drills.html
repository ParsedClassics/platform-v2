As a remedy for poor mastering of morphology professor William C. Dowling recommends to copy by hand certain morphology tables as many times as needed until the knowledge of paradigms develops into automatism. Here you will find morphology tables for Attic Greek, Demotic Greek and Latin in which you can type answers; the anwers will be marked as right or wrong by clicking the button “Check”.
